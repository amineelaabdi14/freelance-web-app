<h1>Service.Ma</h1>
<span style="font-style: italic;">Service.ma is Morocco's first app that connects users with trusted service providers for various tasks. Our mission is to make it easy for people to find reliable and affordable help for their everyday needs. We're committed to partnering with the best service providers in Morocco and supporting local businesses to create job opportunities. Thank you for choosing Service.ma!</span>
<h3 style="margin-top:2rem;">Users:</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Buyer: a user who is looking to purchase services</li>
    <li>Seller: a user who is offering their  services for sale</li>
    <li>Admin: a user who manages the platform, including managing users, projects and roles</li>
</ul>
